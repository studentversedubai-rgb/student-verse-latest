StudentVerse FAQs

Get quick answers to your questions about StudentVerse and saving as a student.

What exactly is StudentVerse?
StudentVerse turns your student ID into SV Pay;tap to pay, get instant discounts, and make your student status work for you.
Is StudentVerse free to join?
StudentVerse Starter is free with 5 redemptions. Upgrade to Pro or Pro+ for more perks, cashback, and exclusive features.
Who can join StudentVerse?
StudentVerse is for university students, verifying your status to unlock exclusive discounts and financial tools.
When will StudentVerse be available?
Launching Q1 2026 with a viral waitlist and MVP app featuring manual redemptions, before full automation later in the year.
How does StudentVerse benefit my student life?
StudentVerse gives instant discounts, smart budgeting, activity planning, and rewards to unlock premium perks.
